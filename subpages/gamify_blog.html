How gamification can be a powerful learning tool

What makes gamification special ?

Gamificiation allows us to tackle tough concepts by inserting them into familiar and approachable applications, this is because games often have a very tight reward feedback loop. Meaning the user is gradually rewarded for their efforts towards an overall goal.

Additionally due to games being an interactive medium this helps form stronger relationships within the brain when accomplishing the task as we can actively test a user's knowledge or skill.
